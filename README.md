# Pharmacy Revenue & Demand Pattern Analysis 
### 6-Year Demand & Revenue Patterns (SQL + Dashboard)

---

## TL;DR

- Analyzed **6 years of pharmacy sales data**
- Identified strong **seasonal + weekly demand patterns**
- Found **Paracetamol drives ~50% of total revenue**
- Demand is **highly predictable, not random**
- Key opportunity: **inventory + staffing optimization**

---

## Business Problem

Pharmacy demand is often managed through intuition, leading to:
- Stock inefficiencies
- Missed seasonal demand spikes
- Poor staffing allocation

This project replaces assumptions with data-driven demand patterns.

---

## Key Insights

- **Revenue concentration risk:** Paracetamol ≈ 50% of total sales  
- **Seasonality:** Strong winter spike (Oct–Jan)  
- **Product cycles:** Allergy meds peak in spring  
- **Operational pattern:** Saturdays outperform all weekdays  
- **System behavior:** High-demand products drive overall sales trends  

---

## Business Impact

- Improve **inventory planning (seasonal stocking)**
- Optimize **staff scheduling (weekends vs weekdays)**
- Reduce **stockout risk for key products**
- Enable **predictive demand planning instead of reactive ordering**
